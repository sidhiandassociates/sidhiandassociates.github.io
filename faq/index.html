⚡ Quick Answer: Submit application on GST portal with PAN, Aadhaar, and business documents

Step 1: Visit GST Portal

• Click "Services" → "Registration" → "New Registration"

Step 2: Part A - Basic Details
• Enter PAN number
• Mobile number & Email
• Verify OTP
• Receive TRN (Temporary Reference Number)

• Business details (name, address, type)
• Promoter/Partner details
• Bank account details
• Principal place of business

Step 4: Upload Documents
• PAN card
• Aadhaar card
• Business address proof

Step 5: Verification
• Sign with DSC (Digital Signature) or
• EVC (e-Verification Code)

⚖️ What is the difference between Tax Audit and Statutory Audit?
⚡ Quick Answer: Tax Audit for Income Tax, Statutory Audit for Companies Act compliance
Tax Audit vs Statutory Audit - Key Differences:

📊 TAX AUDIT (Section 44AB):
Who needs it:
• Business turnover exceeds ₹1 crore (₹10 crore for certain cases)
• Professional receipts exceed ₹50 lakhs
• Presumptive taxation with lower profit declaration

Purpose: Verify accounts for Income Tax compliance
Governed by: Income Tax Act, 1961
Report: Form 3CA/3CB + 3CD
Due Date: October 31st
Our Charges: ₹12,000-₹25,000

Who needs it:
• ALL Private Limited Companies
• ALL LLPs (if turnover > ₹40L or capital > ₹25L)

Due Date: September 30th
