Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cost of LLP registration in India?

Our standard package costs ‚Çπ12,999 for up to 4 partners with additional features like GST support and MSME registration.
How long does LLP registration take?
LLP registration typically takes 7-10 working days from the date of document submission. This includes name approval (1-2 days), DSC & DIN processing (2-3 days), and incorporation filing with MCA (3-5 days). We ensure timely completion with regular status updates.
What documents are required for LLP registration?
Required documents include: PAN cards and Aadhaar cards of all partners, recent photographs, address proof, registered office address proof (rent agreement/ownership documents), utility bill, NOC from property owner, and consent letters from designated partners.
What is the minimum requirement for LLP formation?
Minimum 2 partners are required (maximum unlimited). At least 2 designated partners who must be individuals, and one of them must be an Indian resident. There is no minimum capital requirement - you can start an LLP with any amount of capital contribution.
What are the benefits of LLP over partnership firm?
LLP provides limited liability protection (personal assets protected), separate legal entity status, perpetual succession, professional credibility, easier fundraising, no personal liability for partner's wrongful acts, and better legal standing. Traditional partnership firms don't offer these protections.
Can foreigners be partners in an LLP?
Yes, foreign nationals and foreign companies can be partners in an Indian LLP. However, at least one designated partner must be an Indian resident. Foreign partners need to obtain DSC and comply with FEMA regulations for capital contribution.
What is the annual compliance for LLP?
Annual compliances include: Form 11 (Annual Return) due by May 30th, Form 8 (Statement of Account & Solvency) due by October 30th, Income Tax Return (ITR-5) by September 30th. If turnover exceeds ‚Çπ40 lakhs or contribution exceeds ‚Çπ25 lakhs, statutory audit is also required.
Can I convert my partnership firm to LLP?
Yes, existing partnership firms can be converted to LLP. The conversion process involves filing forms with MCA, obtaining consent from all partners, settling all liabilities, and completing registration formalities. We provide complete assistance for partnership to LLP conversion.
What is LLP agreement and is it mandatory?
LLP agreement is a document defining rights, duties, and obligations of partners. It covers profit sharing, capital contribution, management roles, admission/retirement of partners, and dispute resolution. While not legally mandatory to file with ROC, it's highly recommended to have one to avoid future disputes.
Can I register LLP from home address?
Yes, you can register LLP using your home address as the registered office. You need to provide address proof (utility bill, rent agreement if rented, or property documents if owned) and NOC from the property owner. Many entrepreneurs start with home-based LLPs.
