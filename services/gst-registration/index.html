Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cost of GST registration?
GST registration is FREE from the government - there are no official fees. Our professional service fee is ‚Çπ3,000 for new registration, which includes complete application preparation, document verification, online submission, tracking, and post-registration support.
Who needs GST registration?
GST registration is mandatory if: (1) Annual turnover exceeds ‚Çπ40 lakhs for goods (‚Çπ20 lakhs for services), (2) Making inter-state supply of any value, (3) E-commerce sellers, (4) Casual/non-resident taxable persons.
How long does GST registration take?
GST registration typically takes 3-7 working days if all documents are in order. Process: Submit documents (Day 0), we verify and prepare application (Day 1), submit online and get ARN (Day 2), department processes (Day 3-5), GSTIN issued (Day 5-7).
What documents are required for GST registration?
Required documents: (1) PAN card (mandatory), (2) Aadhaar card of authorized signatory, (3) Business address proof (rent agreement/electricity bill/property papers), (4) Bank account details (cancelled cheque/statement), (5) Passport size photographs, (6) Email and mobile number.
What is composition scheme in GST?
Composition scheme is a simplified GST option for small businesses with turnover up to ‚Çπ1.5 crores. Benefits: (1) Pay tax at lower rates (1%/2%/5%/6%), (2) File returns quarterly instead of monthly, (3) Minimal compliance, (4) Reduced accounting burden.
Can I get GST registration with home address?
Yes, you can register GST with your home/residential address as business address. You need to provide: (1) Address proof (electricity bill/property papers), (2) Self-declaration or NOC from owner if rented, (3) Consent letter if shared premises.
