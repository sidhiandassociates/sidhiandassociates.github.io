What is the difference between bookkeeping and accounting?
Bookkeeping is the day-to-day recording of financial transactions - sales, purchases, receipts, and payments. Accounting involves interpreting, classifying, analyzing, reporting, and summarizing financial data. We provide both services - accurate bookkeeping combined with strategic accounting insights to help you make better business decisions.

You receive monthly financial statements (Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow) by the 10th of each month. Real-time dashboard access is available 24/7 for instant insights. We can also provide weekly or custom reports as needed.
