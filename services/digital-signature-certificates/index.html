Frequently Asked Questions

What is Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)?
Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) is a secure digital key issued by government-approved Certifying Authorities for validating your identity in electronic transactions. It contains your identity information encrypted with a private key that only you possess. DSC is legally equivalent to a physical signature under the Information Technology Act, 2000 and is mandatory for various government filings like Income Tax returns (for audited entities), GST returns, MCA filings, company registration, and participating in e-tenders.
How much does DSC cost in India?
DSC costs vary by type and validity period: Class 2 DSC Individual costs ‚Çπ899 for 1 year or ‚Çπ1,299 for 2 years. Class 2 DSC Organization costs ‚Çπ1,199 for 1 year or ‚Çπ1,699 for 2 years. Class 3 DSC costs ‚Çπ1,799 for 1 year or ‚Çπ2,499 for 2 years. DGFT DSC costs ‚Çπ1,499 for 1 year or ‚Çπ1,999 for 2 years. All packages include USB token, installation support, and technical assistance. There are no hidden charges.
How long does it take to get DSC?
DSC is issued within 1 working day after document verification and video KYC. Class 2 DSC Individual can be processed in 4-6 hours for urgent requirements. Class 2 Organization and Class 3 DSC take approximately 24 hours. DGFT DSC takes 1-2 working days due to additional verification. We provide express service for urgent needs. The DSC is delivered on USB token via courier, or you can download it digitally if needed urgently.
What documents are required for DSC?
For Individual DSC, you need: PAN card (mandatory), Aadhaar card for address proof, passport size photograph with white background, active mobile number and email ID. For Organization DSC, you need: Certificate of Incorporation, company PAN card, authorized signatory's PAN and Aadhaar, board resolution authorizing DSC issuance, and registered office address proof (utility bill or rental agreement). All documents can be submitted online via WhatsApp (+91 78892 57045) or email.
Is DSC mandatory for Income Tax filing?
DSC is mandatory for companies, LLPs, and firms required to get their accounts audited under Income Tax Act (turnover/gross receipts above specified limits). Individual taxpayers and small businesses without audit requirements can file ITR using Aadhaar OTP or EVC (Electronic Verification Code). However, DSC makes e-filing faster, more secure, and is essential for businesses filing tax audit reports, transfer pricing reports, and multiple returns. It's also mandatory for uploading Form 10F, Form 67, and various other tax forms.
Can I use the same DSC for Income Tax, GST, and MCA?
Yes, a single Class 2 DSC can be used for Income Tax e-filing, GST returns, MCA filings, ROC compliance, TDS returns, and most government portals. You don't need separate DSCs for different purposes. However, for government e-tenders, you need Class 3 DSC. For DGFT portal and import-export related filings, you need DGFT-specific DSC. One Class 2 DSC covers 90% of business compliance needs.
What is the difference between Class 2 and Class 3 DSC?
Class 2 DSC provides medium-level security and is used for Income Tax, GST, MCA filings, and general business documentation. It verifies identity against a trusted database. Class 3 DSC provides the highest level of security with in-person verification or video KYC by registration authority. It's mandatory for participating in government e-tenders, e-procurement portals, and high-value transactions. Class 3 costs more (‚Çπ1,799 vs ‚Çπ899) but is required for tender participation. Most businesses only need Class 2 DSC.
How do I renew my DSC after expiry?
DSC renewal should be done before expiry to avoid service disruption. We send renewal reminders 30 days before expiry. To renew, you need to submit updated documents (PAN, Aadhaar, photo) and complete video KYC verification. Renewal costs are same as new DSC (‚Çπ899-‚Çπ1,799 depending on type). You can continue using the same USB token - we'll install the renewed certificate on it. Renewal process takes 1 day. It's advisable to renew 15 days before expiry to ensure continuity.
What is USB token and why is it needed?
USB token is a physical security device (looks like a pen drive) that stores your Digital Signature Certificate securely. It protects your private key with hardware-level encryption, preventing unauthorized access even if someone gets physical access to the token (as it's PIN-protected). USB token is safer than storing DSC on computer as it's immune to viruses and hacking. It's portable and works on any computer. We provide genuine USB token free with every DSC. The token has 3-5 years physical life and can store renewed DSCs.
Can I use DSC on multiple computers?
Yes, you can use the same DSC (USB token) on multiple computers. Simply plug the USB token into any computer, install the token driver software (we provide), and you can sign documents. The DSC is not tied to a specific computer. However, you can use it on only one computer at a time (wherever the token is plugged in). This makes it very convenient for professionals who work from multiple locations or devices. We provide complete installation support for all your computers.
