Frequently Asked Questions

What is the deadline for filing income tax returns?
The deadline for filing ITR for individuals is typically July 31st of the assessment year. For businesses requiring audit, the deadline is usually September 30th. However, we recommend filing early to avoid last-minute rush and claim refunds faster. Belated returns can be filed with late fees.
How much do your tax filing services cost?
Our tax filing services start at ‚Çπ2,500 for simple individual ITR-1 returns. For ITR-2/3 with multiple income sources, the fee is ‚Çπ5,000-8,000. Business returns (ITR-3) start at ‚Çπ8,000, and corporate returns (ITR-6) start at ‚Çπ15,000. The exact fee depends on the complexity of your case.
What documents do I need for ITR filing?
For salaried individuals, you need Form 16, bank statements, investment proofs (80C, 80D), PAN card, and Aadhaar. For business income, you need books of accounts, P&L statement, balance sheet, GST returns, TDS certificates, and bank statements. We provide a detailed checklist based on your income sources.
Can you help me save tax legally?
Yes! Tax planning is our specialty. We analyze your income, identify eligible deductions under sections 80C, 80D, 80E, 80G, HRA exemptions, home loan interest, and more. We also help you choose between old and new tax regimes to minimize your tax liability legally.
What if I missed the ITR filing deadline?
You can still file a belated return before December 31st of the assessment year with a late fee of ‚Çπ5,000 (‚Çπ1,000 if income is below ‚Çπ5 lakhs). We help you file belated returns and minimize penalties. However, you won't be able to carry forward certain losses.
How long does the ITR filing process take?
Once we receive all your documents, we typically complete and file your ITR within 24-48 hours. For complex cases with multiple income sources or business returns, it may take 3-5 working days. We keep you updated throughout the process.
Do you handle income tax notices?
Yes, we provide complete support for income tax notices including scrutiny notices, demand notices, and assessment proceedings. We analyze the notice, prepare appropriate responses, gather supporting documents, and represent you before tax authorities if required.
What is the difference between old and new tax regime?
The old tax regime allows various deductions and exemptions (80C, 80D, HRA, etc.) with higher tax rates. The new regime offers lower tax rates but without most deductions. We analyze your income and investments to recommend which regime saves you more tax.
Can I file ITR if I don't have Form 16?
Yes, you can file ITR without Form 16 using your salary slips, bank statements showing salary credits, and Form 26AS which shows TDS deducted. We help you calculate your taxable income accurately even without Form 16.
How do I track my income tax refund?
After filing your ITR, you can track refund status on the income tax e-filing portal or NSDL website using your PAN and acknowledgment number. Refunds are typically processed within 2-3 months. We provide complete assistance in tracking and following up on your refund.
