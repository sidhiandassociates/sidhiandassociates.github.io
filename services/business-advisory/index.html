Frequently Asked Questions

What is business advisory and how can it help my company? +
Business advisory involves providing expert guidance on strategic, financial, and operational matters to help businesses achieve their goals. We help you identify growth opportunities, optimize financial performance, improve operational efficiency, manage risks, and navigate complex business challenges. Our services are valuable for startups seeking direction, growing businesses needing structure, or established companies looking to expand or transform.
What's the difference between a business advisor and a consultant? +
While both provide expert guidance, a business advisor typically builds long-term relationships and provides ongoing strategic support across multiple business areas. Consultants often focus on specific projects or problems. As your business advisors, we act as trusted partners who understand your business deeply and provide continuous guidance on strategy, finance, operations, and growth - similar to having an experienced board member or mentor.
Do I need a virtual CFO if I already have an accountant? +
Yes, they serve different purposes. Accountants typically handle bookkeeping, tax compliance, and historical financial reporting. A virtual CFO provides strategic financial leadership - forecasting, budgeting, financial analysis, cash flow management, investor relations, and strategic decision support. Think of an accountant as looking at past finances, while a CFO focuses on future financial strategy and business growth.
