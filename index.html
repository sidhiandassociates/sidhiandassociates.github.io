How to register a Private Limited company in India online?+
Sidhi & Associates registers your Pvt Ltd company in 7–15 working days from ₹7,500. What documents are required for ITR filing?+
Required: Form 16 (salaried), Form 16A/B/C (other TDS), PAN card, Aadhaar card, bank statements, investment proofs (80C: PPF/ELSS/LIC; 80D: health insurance), property/rental income details, capital gains statements, Form 26AS/AIS/TIS from the income tax portal. Our CA sends a personalised checklist after an initial call.
Can I use Sidhi & Associates services online from anywhere in India?+
Yes. We provide virtual CA services Pan-India. Share documents via WhatsApp, email, or Client Portal. All services — ITR, GST, company registration, audit, bookkeeping — handled remotely. Contact: +91 78892 57045 / siddhiandassociatess@gmail.com.
What happens if I miss the ITR filing deadline?+
Missing the July 31, 2026 deadline triggers a late fee under Section 234F: ₹1,000 (income below ₹5 lakh) or ₹5,000. Additionally: interest under Sec 234A/234B/234C, loss of capital loss carry-forward, and difficulty in loan/visa applications. File early — Sidhi & Associates ITR filing from ₹1,000. Call +91 78892 57045.
What is the difference between ITR-1 (Sahaj) and ITR-4 (Sugam)?+
ITR-1 (Sahaj): for salaried individuals with income up to ₹50 lakh from salary, one house property, and other sources. ITR-4 (Sugam): for individuals/HUFs/firms with presumptive income under Sec 44AD, 44ADA, or 44AE (small businesses and professionals). Our CA assesses your profile and recommends the right form. Call +91 78892 57045.
Who needs GST registration in India?+
GST registration is mandatory if: annual turnover exceeds ₹40 lakh (₹20 lakh for services), you make inter-state supplies, you are an e-commerce seller, you act as an agent/broker, or you are a casual taxable person. Sidhi & Associates handles GST registration from ₹1,500. Call +91 78892 57045 for an eligibility check.
