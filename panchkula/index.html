How long does company incorporation take? ▼

Company incorporation typically takes 7-10 working days from the date of submission of all required documents. This includes name approval, DIN/DSC application, MOA/AOA filing, and incorporation certificate. Company Registration starts at ₹7,500.

What documents are needed for ITR filing? ▼

For salaried individuals: Form 16, salary slips, bank statements, investment proofs, Form 26AS, and PAN card. For business income: Books of accounts, bank statements, purchase/sales invoices, GST returns, and Form 26AS. We'll guide you through the exact documents needed based on your income sources.

Do you provide tax planning services? ▼

Yes, we provide comprehensive tax planning services to help you minimize tax liability legally. We advise on investments under Section 80C, 80D, 80G, HRA optimization, home loan benefits, and other tax-saving strategies. Book a consultation to create a personalized tax plan.

Can I get a home visit for CA services? ▼

Yes, we provide home visit services for clients in Panchkula and nearby areas for an additional charge. This is especially helpful for senior citizens, businesses requiring on-site work, or those with complex documentation. Contact us to schedule a home visit.

What payment methods do you accept? ▼
