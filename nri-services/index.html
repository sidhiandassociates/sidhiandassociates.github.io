Frequently Asked Questions — NRI Tax Services

Answers to the questions we hear most from NRIs and OCIs

What is the cost of ITR filing for NRIs in India?
NRI ITR filing starts at ₹5,000, covering salary, NRE/NRO interest, dividend, rental, and capital gains income. Pricing is flat-rate with no hidden charges. Call +91 78892 57045.
How does DTAA help NRIs avoid double taxation?
The DTAA between India and your country of residence lets you claim credit for tax already paid in one country against your liability in the other, via Form 67. We handle this from ₹4,000.
What is a Lower TDS Certificate and why do I need it when selling property?
Buyers must deduct TDS at 20%+ on the full sale value when buying from an NRI, regardless of your actual gain. A Lower TDS Certificate (Form 13) restricts TDS to your real capital gain. We prepare and file this from ₹15,000.
What is Form 15CA/15CB and when is it required?
Form 15CA (self-declaration) and 15CB (CA certificate) are required before remitting funds outside India — property sale proceeds, dividends, or other income. We provide this from ₹3,000.
Can NRIs repatriate sale proceeds of property from India?
Yes, subject to FEMA limits — generally up to USD 1 million per financial year from NRO balances, subject to tax clearance and bank documentation. We advise on this from ₹10,000.
Do I need to file ITR if TDS has already been deducted?
Yes. Filing is often the only way to claim a refund of excess TDS, especially on property sales and NRO interest. We handle this from ₹5,000.
Do you work with NRIs across different time zones?
Yes — we serve clients across the US, UK, UAE, Canada, Australia and Singapore, with flexible scheduling and international payment options including wire transfer and PayPal.
