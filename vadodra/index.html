What documents do I need for GST registration in Vadodara? +
For GST registration in Vadodara, Gujarat, you need: (1) PAN card of proprietor/partners/directors, (2) Aadhaar card, (3) Business address proof — electricity bill or rent agreement for your Vadodara address, (4) Bank account details with cancelled cheque, (5) Passport-size photograph, (6) Business registration documents (if applicable). Sidhi & Associates completes GST registration in Vadodara within 3-5 working days. WhatsApp your documents to +91 78892 57045.
How to register a Private Limited company in Vadodara? +
To register a Private Limited company in Vadodara, Gujarat: Step 1 — Obtain Digital Signature Certificates (DSC) for all directors. Step 2 — Apply for Director Identification Number (DIN). Step 3 — Check and reserve company name via MCA's RUN portal. Step 4 — File SPICe+ incorporation form with MCA along with MOA and AOA. Step 5 — Receive Certificate of Incorporation. Step 6 — Apply for PAN, TAN, bank account, and GST registration. Sidhi & Associates completes the entire process in 7-10 working days. Call +91 78892 57045.

What is the GST filing due date for businesses in Gujarat? +
For businesses in Gujarat including Vadodara: GSTR-1 (sales return) is due on the 11th of the next month for monthly filers. GSTR-3B (tax payment return) is due on the 20th of the next month. Under QRMP scheme, quarterly due dates apply. GSTR-9 annual return is due on 31st December. Late filing attracts ₹50/day penalty (₹20/day for nil returns).
