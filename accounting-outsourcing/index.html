Is my firm's client data secure when outsourcing to India?
Yes. We use encrypted access to your existing accounting software, signed NDAs and data processing agreements, restricted VPN-based access, and no local storage of client data outside your systems.
Do your staff know US GAAP and UK GAAP?
Yes, our staff are trained specifically in US GAAP and UK GAAP conventions and are experienced with QuickBooks Online/Desktop, Xero, and Zoho Books.
Can outsourced staff help during US tax season?
Yes. We provide seasonal scaling of tax prep support for 1040, 1120, and 1065 workpaper preparation during Jan-April, and for UK Self Assessment and VAT deadlines.
Dedicated bookkeeper vs. per-task pricing — what's the difference?
A dedicated bookkeeper or staff accountant works exclusively for your firm on a fixed monthly or hourly basis, learning your workflows over time. Per-task pricing suits occasional, smaller volumes. We offer both.
What time zone overlap can I expect?
Our teams work flexible shifts to overlap with US Eastern/Pacific and UK working hours for daily check-ins, with async work continuing outside overlap hours.
Which software platforms do you support?
QuickBooks Online and Desktop, Xero, Zoho Books, and we can work within your existing practice management and workpaper software as needed.
